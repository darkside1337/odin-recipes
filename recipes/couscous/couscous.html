How to prepare Garantita

Ingrediants:

  • 1¾ cups low sodium chicken or vegetable broth (or water)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1½ cups (10 oz) couscous

Preparations:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the water (or broth), salt, butter, and oil to a boil.
  2. Stir in the couscous, cover tightly with a lid, and remove from heat.
  3. Let the couscous steam for 5 minutes.
  4. Use a fork to fluff the couscous and break up any clumps. Serve warm.
